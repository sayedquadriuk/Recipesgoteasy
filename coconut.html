Spicy Coconut Noodles

Ingredients

  1. 8 ounces rice noodles or fettuccine
  2. 13.5-ounce can unsweetened coconut milk
  3. tablespoons tomato paste
  4. teaspoon chili powder
  5. teaspoon kosher salt
  6. tablespoon chili paste or sauce (optional)
  7. scallions, thinly sliced
  8. 1cup vegetable or chicken broth
  9. ounces bean sprouts
  10. 2 bay leaves
  11. basil leaves, whole or torn
  12. cup shredded coconut, toasted

DIRECTIONS

Cook the noodles according to the package directions. Drain and set aside. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, over medium-high heat, combine the coconut milk, tomato paste, chili powder, salt, and chili paste (if desired). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir the drained noodles into the sauce and toss. Divide among individual bowls. Top with the scallions, sprouts, basil, and coconut (if desired).
